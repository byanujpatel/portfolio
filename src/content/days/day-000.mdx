**Day 0/100**

I don't fear failure.

I fear **rejection**.

A video not recorded.

A DM not sent.

An application not submitted.

Today I watched Jia Jiang's **100 Days of Rejection**.

One lesson stayed with me:

**Stay engaged.**

**Don't run.**

**Just ask.**

So I'm starting my own **100-Day Fear Challenge**.

One uncomfortable action.

Every day.

Not to get more **Yeses**.

To stop letting fear decide.

Day 0 is live.

See you on Day 1.

## What happened

Today, I reached out to successful founders.

I sent personalized messages instead of generic templates. My goal wasn't to ask for a job or a favor—it was to learn.

Some founders didn't reply.

Some did.

The conversations gave me practical insights into how they build AI products, think about production systems, and solve real-world problems.

## What I learned

Most people are willing to help when you're genuine.

Don't fear being ignored.

Send the message.

Every conversation is an opportunity to learn.

What happened
Today, I recorded and shared my first face video.

For a long time, I avoided being on camera because I was worried about what people might think. I kept waiting until I felt more confident.

Today, I decided not to wait.

I recorded the video, accepted that it wouldn't be perfect, and published it anyway.

What I learned

Fear gets smaller once you take action.

Confidence doesn't come first.

Action does.

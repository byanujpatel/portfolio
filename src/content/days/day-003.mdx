## What happened

Today, I challenged the belief that I'm "not experienced enough."

Instead of waiting until I felt more qualified, I reached out to people whose work I genuinely admire—podcasters, DevRel professionals, and engineers who have expanded beyond engineering into product building, GTM, and content.

I introduced myself, shared the AI projects I've built, explained what I'm currently learning, and asked if they'd be open to a conversation.

Most didn't reply.

One did.

Tomorrow, I'll be speaking with Naman for 30 minutes to learn from his journey and exchange ideas.

## What I learned

You don't need to be 100% ready before reaching out.

The biggest rejection often comes from yourself.

Give people the chance to say yes or no.

Sometimes, one genuine message is all it takes to create a new opportunity.
